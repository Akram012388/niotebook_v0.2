# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive environment for everyone contributing to Niotebook. Expected behavior includes:

- Using welcoming and professional language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, and any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Reporting

To report a concern, email the maintainers at **niotebook@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate these standards. Participants who engage in unacceptable behavior may be removed from the project at the maintainers' discretion.

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, and any other communication channels used by the project.
